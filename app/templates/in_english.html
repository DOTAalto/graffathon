Graffathon in a nutshell

Graffathon is a hackathon focused on learning graphics programming by trying it out hands on. It is the sequel to the popular event Code+Audio+Graphics, which was held last year. This year we strive to make the event even more beginner-friendly by offering more presentations and workshops that focus on the practical skills needed to create computer graphics. You can sign up for the event at the signup page (Ilmoittaudu)!

To participate you only need basic programming skills (from any language) and math (basic courses from school for example) - you do not have to be able to code graphics, make music, draw or program. Just bring a computer and something to eat. You will learn. We also offer some snacks for the price of entry. The computer does not have to be the latest and greatest, you do not need a crazy graphics card to try out graphics programming. Almost any computer will do.

Location

The event takes place at the App Campus facilities in Aalto Innovation House and the address is Otaniementie 19-21. The map on the right illustrates the location. The entrance to App Campus can be found at the left end of the house (seen from the street). Further instructions will be found at the door.

App Campus has two well-equipped kitchens (oven included), a gas grill at the balcony, free coffee, showers, rooms for brainstorming, workspace and a presentation room. See the pictures below.

There are free parking slots in case you are arriving by car. You can get there easily by public transport as well, buses from Espoo, Helsinki and Vantaa bring you to 500 meters from the site.

FAQ

What do people do at the event?

Code, socialize, listen to presentations and at the end enjoy the amazing compo show. In addition, there will be little fun stuff to do during the whole event in case you hit a creative block.

What do I need to know to participate?

You should have a very basic understanding of programming and know some simple mathematics (mostly trigonometry, linear algebra (=vectors) and matrices for 3d-graphics). That's all! Don't worry, there will be helpful people around and the presentations will aid you in getting started!

Why do you charge money for the event?

Good question! Code+Audio+Graphics, which we organized last year, was free but this year we wanted to make the event even better. The entrance fee includes perks like free coffee and fruits. A part of the admissions will be paid back to the visitors in form of good prizes. The rest of the money will be used to renew the DOT library so you will be supporting learning as well!

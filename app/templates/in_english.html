Graffathon is a hackathon focused on learning graphics programming by trying it out hands on. It is the sequel to the popular event Code+Audio+Graphics, which was held last year. This year we strive to make the event even more beginner-friendly by offering more presentations and workshops that focus on the practical skills needed to create computer graphics.

To participate you only need basic programming skills (from any language) and math (basic courses from school for example) - you do not have to be able to code graphics, make music, draw or program. Just bring a computer and something to eat. You will learn. We also offer some snacks for the price of entry. The computer does not have to be the latest and greatest, you do not need a crazy graphics card to try out graphics programming. Almost any computer will do.

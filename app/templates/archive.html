Entries

Total of 19 demos, 4 drawn pictures and 5 modeling entries were released at Graffathon. Click the links below to find more info or scroll down to photos.

Links

results.txtThe final voting results.
Scene.orgDownload all productions! Demos are available under demo/ as zipfiles which contain 32- and 64-bit Windows executables and sources (for running on Mac/Linux). You can also download all graphics (gfx/) and modeling (modeling/) entries as zip-packages.
Pouet.netAll demos were added to Pouet.net, the main database of demoscene productions. Create an account if you don't already have one and leave a comment!
ArtCityYou can easily view all graphics and modeling entries at ArtCity, the Demoscene Graphics Gallery. Again, remember to leave comment :)
GithubGrab sources of all demos from Github for learning and experimentation.

Are you lazy and don't want to watch demos real-time on your computer? No worries! The Youtube-playlist attached below contains video captures of all demos in the order of final results.
